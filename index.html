Chapter 0: The Overripe Mango Background

A quirky tale about African societal expectations for women who are of "marrying age"

Mwayi reached the age of being called "aunty" by the kids at family events. Rightfully so, since she began to have the height and posture of one, at the ripe age of 32.

She has a mile long Resume certifying her to be a responsible young adult. Yet, none of this was sufficient for her mother's ever imposing and opinionated friends, who she called (in her mind, of course) The Marriage Monitoring Aunties Association, a funny title which was inspired by her favorite book, who also loudly nicknamed her "The Overripe Mango".
