Chapter 2: Hit the Fan

Once home from the most embarrassing Thanksgiving dinner of her life, Mwayi rushed to her room before her mother could ask her any questions. Flopping herself on the bed, she stared at her fan, wondering what overtook her to spew such a bold lie. As the fan turned, so did the gears in her brain for how she could escape the mental prison she has put herself in to bear the burden of her society's expectations. She grabbed her phone and facetimed her best friend, Alinafe, known to her closest companions as Nafe. Surely she would have an answer for her dilemma with a ticking one and a half month timer on it. Nafe could not believe her ears when Mwayi narrated her predicament. "So chomie (friend), what are you going to do?" Mwayi exclaimed, "Girl, that is why I called you! We have less than 6 weeks to figure something out!" Nafe pondered, then lit up. "Oh no, I do not like that look,’ Mwayi said. Nafe slyly said, "You trust me, don’t you?
